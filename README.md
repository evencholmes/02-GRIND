# 02 GRIND
 The habit tracker [a bit mean]

I built GRIND because every habit app out there is too nice to you.

"Great job! 🌟 You showed up today!" yeah, you did the bare minimum, congratulations.

GRIND doesn't clap for you. It just watches. And it remembers. Every streak you broke. Every day you said "I'll start Monday" and didn't. It's all there. No sugar coating.

Your data stays on your device. No account, no cloud, no company quietly selling your "wellness journey" to advertisers. Export a .grind backup regularly unlike your discipline, that part is actually your responsibility.

I'm not on the Play Store. Costs $25 to get listed and I'm an intern making ~$30 a month. Do the math. This is a pet project. I built it for myself because I was tired of apps that wanted my data, my subscription, or my soul.

If GRIND is making you show up when you don't want to a dollar. That's it. Only if you mean it, only if you can.

Can't spare a dollar? Fine. But if this app is actually helping you get off your ass tell someone. Costs nothing. Means everything.

Built with obsession. Free by choice.

No excuses accepted.
