ABOUT GRIND

GRIND v2.0 — Habit OS

All data stored in your device's localStorage and IndexedDB. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your discipline is yours alone.

App Lock is a convenience feature only — it is not a security product. Use your device's screen lock as primary protection.

Export a .grind backup regularly.
